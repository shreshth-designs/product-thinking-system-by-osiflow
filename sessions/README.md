# Sessions

Save outputs from your product thinking runs here.

One file per session. Name them: `YYYY-MM-DD-topic.md`

Example: `2026-05-23-osiflow-hmi-brief.md`

---

## What to save

After running any command or agent, paste the key output here:
- The reframed problem statement
- The assumptions Claude flagged
- The decision you made and why
- What you're NOT building (and why)

This becomes your product thinking audit trail.
